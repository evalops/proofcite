FAA UAS Operations (excerpt)
Maximum altitude is 400 feet AGL unless within 400 feet of a structure.
Maintain visual line of sight (VLOS) with the aircraft at all times.
Do not fly over people or moving vehicles.
Do not operate in controlled airspace without authorization.
Yield right of way to manned aircraft.
Do not operate under the influence of drugs or alcohol.
Night operations require appropriate anti-collision lighting.
